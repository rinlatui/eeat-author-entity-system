Use official documentation and live validation tools when implementing schema and author systems.

## Practical Notes

- Do not create fake authors.
- Do not create fake reviewer identities.
- Do not use schema to claim expertise that is not visible on the page.
- sameAs links should point to real public profiles.
- Author and reviewer information should help users verify who is responsible for the content.
